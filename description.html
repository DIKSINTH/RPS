Rock Paper Scissor

The Rock, Paper, Scissors game is one of the simplest and most well-known hand games played worldwide. The Rock, Paper, Scissors game is a simple hand game usually played between two people. It's a classic decision-making tool, often used to resolve conflicts, make choices, or just for fun.

RULES OF THE GAME

Each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with their hand:
1. Rock (✊) A closed fist.
2. Paper (✋) An open hand.
3. Scissors (✌) A fist with the index and middle fingers extended.

The winner is determined by the following rules:

Rock beats Scissors (Rock crushes Scissors).
Scissors beats Paper (Scissors cut Paper).
Paper beats Rock (Paper wraps Rock).
If both players choose the same shape, it's a tie, and they play again.
